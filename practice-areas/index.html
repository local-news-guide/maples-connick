Practice Areas

We concentrate on high-stakes cases where preparation, expertise, and trial readiness make the difference. Four focused practice areas across three states.

Personal Injury & Negligence

Catastrophic injury and wrongful death litigation for victims of negligence. We handle auto accidents, truck collisions, workplace injuries, premises liability, products liability, and wrongful death claims across Louisiana, Alabama, and Georgia.

Our attorneys prepare every case for trial. Insurance companies know the difference between firms that settle and firms that try cases. That difference shows up in the offers they make.

  • Auto and truck accident litigation
  • Workplace and industrial injuries
  • Wrongful death and survival actions
  • Premises liability and slip-and-fall
  • Products liability

Medical Malpractice

Holding negligent healthcare providers accountable for surgical errors, misdiagnosis, medication mistakes, birth injuries, anesthesia errors, and hospital negligence. We work with leading medical experts to build compelling cases.

Medical malpractice cases require both legal skill and medical understanding. We retain board-certified experts in the relevant specialty to establish the standard of care and prove how it was breached.

  • Surgical errors and hospital negligence
  • Misdiagnosis and delayed diagnosis
  • Medication and anesthesia errors
  • Birth injuries and obstetric negligence
  • Emergency room malpractice

Nursing Home Negligence

Protecting vulnerable elders from institutional abuse, neglect, and substandard care. We pursue accountability from nursing facilities, corporate owners, and the systems that fail our most vulnerable residents.

Nursing home cases often involve understaffing, inadequate training, and corporate cost-cutting that puts profits over patient safety. We investigate the full chain of responsibility, from bedside staff to the boardroom.

  • Elder abuse and physical neglect
  • Bedsore and fall injuries
  • Medication errors in care facilities
  • Understaffing and corporate negligence
  • Wrongful death in nursing homes

Immigration law is personal. Our attorneys understand the urgency of status issues, the fear of removal proceedings, and the hope that drives families through the process. We handle every case with the care it deserves.

  • Employment visas (H-1B, L-1, O-1)

Our Process

How We Approach Cases

Every case follows a disciplined process designed to build the strongest possible position, whether the case resolves through negotiation or goes to trial.

Free Case Evaluation
We start with a thorough review of your situation. You tell us what happened, we listen carefully, ask the right questions, and give you an honest assessment of your legal options. There is no charge and no obligation.
Deep Investigation
We gather medical records, accident reports, expert opinions, and witness statements. We retain specialists in the relevant field to establish the standard of care, reconstruct events, and quantify damages. Every case is built as if it will go to trial.
Negotiation or Trial
We demand full and fair compensation. When insurance companies or defendants refuse reasonable offers, we are prepared to take your case to a jury. Our willingness to try cases creates leverage that settlement-only firms cannot match.
Resolution & Recovery
Once compensation is secured, we handle all liens, subrogation, medical provider negotiations, and distributions. You receive a clear accounting of every dollar. Our job is not done until you are made whole.

Multi-State Representation

Different states, different laws, different courts. We know the distinctions that matter.

Louisiana

Headquartered in New Orleans, our firm has deep roots in Louisiana's civil law tradition. We handle cases across the state, from the Greater New Orleans area to Baton Rouge and beyond.

Louisiana's legal system is unique in the United States, blending French civil law with common law principles. Our attorneys understand the state's Medical Malpractice Act, comparative fault rules, and prescriptive periods that differ from every other state.

New Orleans office

Alabama

Our Mobile office serves clients throughout Alabama. We handle personal injury, nursing home negligence, and industrial accident cases statewide.

Alabama's contributory negligence doctrine, one of the strictest in the nation, demands careful case preparation. A single misstep can bar recovery entirely. Our attorneys know how to build cases that survive this standard.

Mobile office

Georgia

Our Atlanta office extends our reach into the Southeast's largest legal market. We represent clients in personal injury, medical malpractice, and immigration cases across Georgia.

Georgia's modified comparative fault system, apportionment statute, and tort reform caps on certain damages require strategic case building from the outset. Our multi-state experience gives clients an advantage that single-jurisdiction firms cannot offer.

Atlanta office

We also handle cases in Mississippi and Tennessee through Of Counsel attorney Ian Brendel.
