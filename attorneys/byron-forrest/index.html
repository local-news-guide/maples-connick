Byron Forrest is a trial attorney at Maples & Connick, LLC, where he focuses on traumatic brain injuries, nursing home negligence, and medical malpractice. He is a passionate advocate for catastrophic injury victims, bringing thorough preparation and determined courtroom presence to every case he handles.

Brain Injury Expertise

Traumatic brain injuries are among the most devastating and complex injuries a person can suffer. Byron understands the medical science behind TBI cases, including the long-term cognitive, emotional, and physical consequences that may not be immediately apparent. He works closely with neurologists, neuropsychologists, and rehabilitation specialists to build comprehensive damage models that capture the true extent of his clients’ losses.

Nursing Home Accountability

In nursing home negligence cases, Byron holds facilities and their corporate owners accountable when residents suffer harm from abuse, neglect, falls, medication errors, or understaffing. These cases demand an attorney willing to investigate institutional practices, review staffing records, and expose systemic failures. Byron approaches each case with the understanding that the vulnerable individuals harmed by negligent care deserve a vigorous legal response.

Your Representation

Byron represents clients across the firm’s offices in New Orleans, Mobile, and Atlanta. He prepares every case as though it will go to trial, because that level of preparation produces the strongest outcomes at every stage of litigation. Consultations are free, and all personal injury cases are handled on a contingency fee basis, meaning clients pay nothing unless Byron recovers compensation on their behalf.
