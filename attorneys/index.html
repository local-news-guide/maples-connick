Founding Members

Aaron Maples and Brendan Connick founded this firm in 2024 because they saw the same problem over and over: injury victims with life-altering cases being pushed into quick settlements by firms that wouldn’t prepare for trial. They built Maples & Connick to be different — a small team that takes fewer cases, invests more in each one, and goes to trial when the other side won’t pay what a case is worth.
