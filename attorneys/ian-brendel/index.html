Ian Brendel

Of Counsel • 5 States

Personal Injury Industrial Accidents Criminal Law Multi-State

Ian Brendel serves as Of Counsel at Maples & Connick, LLC, bringing an exceptionally broad geographic reach to the firm. Licensed to practice in five states — Alabama, Georgia, Mississippi, Louisiana, and Tennessee — Ian handles personal injury, industrial accidents, and criminal law cases across the Gulf South and Southeast.

Ian's multi-state licensure is a significant advantage for clients whose cases involve incidents, parties, or legal issues that cross state lines. Different states have different statutes of limitations, comparative fault rules, damage caps, and procedural requirements. Ian understands these distinctions and knows how to navigate the legal landscape in each jurisdiction to protect his clients' interests.

Industrial accident cases are a particular focus of Ian's practice. Workers injured on oil rigs, construction sites, manufacturing plants, and other industrial environments face complex legal questions involving employer negligence, third-party liability, workers' compensation, and federal maritime or railroad safety laws. Ian investigates the root causes of workplace accidents, identifies every responsible party, and pursues full compensation for medical expenses, lost wages, pain and suffering, and long-term disability.

Ian also maintains an active criminal defense practice, representing clients charged with misdemeanors and felonies across all five states where he is licensed. Whether handling a personal injury claim, an industrial accident case, or a criminal matter, Ian provides direct attorney access, straightforward communication, and determined advocacy. Consultations are free, and personal injury cases are handled on a contingency fee basis.
