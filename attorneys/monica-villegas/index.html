Monica Villegas

Paralegal

Case Management Client Communication Document Preparation

Monica Villegas is a paralegal at Maples & Connick, LLC, where she provides essential support to the firm's attorneys across every stage of litigation. From initial intake through resolution, Monica keeps cases organized, deadlines tracked, and clients informed — the operational backbone that allows the legal team to focus on advocacy.

In a firm that handles catastrophic injury, medical malpractice, and nursing home negligence cases, the volume of records, filings, and correspondence is substantial. Monica manages case files with precision, coordinating medical record requests, organizing discovery materials, preparing court filings, and ensuring that nothing falls through the cracks. Her attention to detail directly supports the thorough case preparation that defines the firm's approach.

Clients at Maples & Connick frequently describe the firm's responsiveness and communication as standout qualities. Monica plays a central role in that experience. She serves as a primary point of contact for clients who have questions about their case status, upcoming deadlines, or documentation needs. When a client calls the office, Monica ensures they receive a timely, informed response.

Monica supports the attorneys across all three of the firm's offices in New Orleans, Mobile, and Atlanta. Her work spans the firm's core practice areas — personal injury, medical malpractice, nursing home negligence, and immigration — and she brings the same level of care and organization to every case regardless of complexity.
