Aaron N. Maples is a founding member of Maples & Connick, LLC, and a passionate trial attorney who has dedicated his career to advocating for catastrophic injury victims. He holds the AV Preeminent rating from Martindale-Hubbell, the highest distinction for legal ability and ethical standards, and has been selected to the Super Lawyers Rising Stars list.

Practice Focus

Aaron concentrates his practice on traumatic brain injuries, nursing home negligence, and medical malpractice. He understands the devastating impact these injuries have on individuals and families, and he brings the preparation, persistence, and courtroom skill necessary to hold negligent parties accountable.

Trial Approach

Aaron does not simply negotiate from behind a desk. He prepares every case with the expectation that it will go before a jury, and this approach produces results. Insurance companies and defense attorneys recognize that Aaron is willing to take a case to verdict, which changes the calculus at every stage of litigation.

Your Representation

Aaron represents clients from the firm’s offices in New Orleans, Mobile, and Atlanta. He is committed to providing each client with direct attorney access, clear communication, and the aggressive representation their case demands. There are no upfront costs — Aaron works on a contingency fee basis.
