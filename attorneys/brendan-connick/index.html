Brendan P. Connick is a founding member of Maples & Connick, LLC, and a trial lawyer dedicated to representing victims of catastrophic personal injury, wrongful death, products liability, and first-party property damage throughout the New Orleans area and across the Gulf South.

Practice Focus

Brendan handles complex, high-value cases that demand thorough investigation, expert coordination, and aggressive courtroom advocacy. Whether the case involves a defective product that caused severe injury, a wrongful death caused by corporate negligence, or a property damage dispute where an insurer has refused to pay, Brendan brings the same level of intensity and preparation.

Products liability cases require an attorney who understands engineering, manufacturing processes, and regulatory standards — Brendan works closely with technical and medical experts to establish how a product failed.

Products & Wrongful Death

Products liability cases require an attorney who understands engineering, manufacturing processes, and regulatory standards. Brendan works closely with technical and medical experts to establish how a product failed and why a manufacturer should be held accountable. In wrongful death litigation, he fights to ensure that families receive full compensation for the loss of a loved one.

Insurance Bad Faith

Brendan represents policyholders against insurance companies that deny, delay, or undervalue legitimate claims. He understands the tactics insurers use and knows how to counter them — whether through negotiation or at trial.

Your Representation

Clients work directly with Brendan and pay nothing upfront. He is committed to direct attorney access, clear communication, and aggressive advocacy at every stage of litigation.
