Chanel Payne

Of Counsel • Immigration

Immigration Personal Injury Family Law Trilingual

Chanel Payne serves as Of Counsel at Maples & Connick, LLC, where she focuses on immigration, personal injury, and family law. Fluent in English, Spanish, and Portuguese, Chanel is committed to providing accessible legal services to the diverse communities the firm serves across Louisiana, Alabama, and Georgia.

Chanel's immigration practice covers the full spectrum of immigration needs, including visa applications, green card petitions, deportation defense, asylum claims, and naturalization. She understands that immigration matters are not just legal proceedings; they are life-defining events that affect families, livelihoods, and futures. Chanel guides clients through every step of the process with clarity and compassion, ensuring they understand their options and their rights.

Her trilingual capabilities allow her to serve clients who may otherwise face language barriers in the legal system. Spanish-speaking and Portuguese-speaking clients can communicate directly with Chanel without the need for interpreters, which improves the accuracy of case preparation and builds the trust that effective representation requires.

In addition to immigration, Chanel handles personal injury and family law matters. This breadth of practice allows her to serve clients with overlapping legal needs, such as an injury victim who also has pending immigration concerns, or a family navigating both custody issues and documentation status. Consultations with Chanel are free, and personal injury cases are handled on a contingency fee basis.
