Our Process

Our Approach to Every Case

Results come from preparation. Every case we accept goes through the same rigorous process.

Thorough Investigation
We reconstruct every incident from the ground up — medical records, accident reports, expert consultations, and witness interviews. No detail is overlooked.
Trial-Ready Preparation
Every case file is built as though it will be presented to a jury. Expert witnesses retained, demonstrative evidence prepared, legal theories researched and briefed.
Aggressive Advocacy
We negotiate from a position of strength. When fair settlement is refused, we take the case to trial. Insurance companies know we mean it.
Full Resolution
We handle all post-resolution work — lien negotiation, subrogation, medical bill reduction, and distribution — so clients receive the maximum net recovery.
