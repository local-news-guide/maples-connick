Common Questions

What Clients Ask Before They Call

Will I ever pay anything out of pocket?

No. We work on contingency — you pay zero upfront and owe us nothing unless we recover money for you. We advance all case costs including expert witnesses, accident reconstruction, medical records, and court fees. If we don’t win, you don’t pay. That’s not a slogan — it’s how every case at this firm works.

What if another law firm already turned down my case?

Call us anyway. We routinely accept complex, high-value cases that other firms decline or refer out. Our signature result — a $4.4 million recovery for a family after an industrial accident — came from a case that three other law firms said no to. Difficult facts and well-funded defendants don’t scare us. That’s what this firm was built for.

Will I actually talk to a lawyer, or will I get passed to a case manager?

You talk to your attorney directly. We take fewer cases so each lawyer can invest more in every client. Your trial attorney knows every detail of your case because they’re the one working it — not delegating it. It’s the single most common thing our clients mention in reviews: “They always took the time to answer my questions.”
