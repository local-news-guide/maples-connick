How It Works

Your Case, Step by Step

1
Free Consultation
Call or fill out our form. We listen to your story, review the facts, and tell you honestly whether you have a case — at no cost to you.
2
Investigation
We gather evidence, consult medical and technical experts, document your injuries and losses, and build a case designed to maximize your recovery.
3
Negotiation or Trial
We pursue full compensation through negotiation. If the other side refuses a fair settlement, we take your case to trial. We prepare every case as if it will be tried.
4
Resolution
You receive your compensation. We handle all liens, subrogation, and distributions so you can focus on recovery.
