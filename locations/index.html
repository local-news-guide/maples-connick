Different Laws

Each state has distinct statutes of limitation, fault systems, and damage caps. Louisiana uses civil law with prescriptive periods. Alabama applies strict contributory negligence. Georgia follows modified comparative fault. We know the distinctions that determine outcomes.

Local Courts

Our attorneys practice regularly in the courts where your case will be heard. We know the local rules, the judges, and the procedural requirements that vary from parish to parish, county to county.

One Firm

Whether your case originates in Louisiana, Alabama, or Georgia, you get the full resources of every office. Our attorneys collaborate across state lines to build the strongest possible case.
